Methodology: Rapid Narrative Review

Data Sources

  • WHO Prequalification & EUL records
  • WHO-Listed Authorities
  • ClinicalTrials.gov & PACTR
  • FIND diagnostics database
  • FDA, EMA, African NRAs

Inclusion Criteria

  • Late-stage clinical development (Phase II/III)
  • WHO-authorized or under review
  • Human safety/efficacy data available
  • Relevant to African outbreak contexts

Key Analytical Lens

  • "Is the MCM decision-ready for Africa?"
  • Disease burden & outbreak frequency
  • Regulatory status (WHO PQ/EUL/LA)
  • Operational feasibility

Expected Outputs

Evidence Briefs

2-5 page rapid evidence assessments per disease

Summary Slide Deck

Decision-maker ready presentation

Manuscript

Full evidence assessment for publication
